About Mark Rothko

Mark Rothko, an American abstract painter, was known for his large-scale color field paintings. His works feature large areas of color with subtle variations in tone, creating an immersive experience for viewers.

Rothko's Artistic Style

Rothko used soft edges and blurred borders, allowing colors to transition smoothly, creating a meditative effect. His work is often interpreted as exploring profound themes of human emotion, spirituality, and existence.
